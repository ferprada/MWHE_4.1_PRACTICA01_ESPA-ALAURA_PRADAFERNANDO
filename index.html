the scoop lamp

Born out of the desire for affordability without sacrificing quality and design, the Analuisa Corrigan x Wooj collaboration was in development for over a year. Analuisa Corrigan, a ceramic artist and designer, and Wooj, a 3D printing lighting design company, married their unique crafts and skillsets to create The Scoop Lamp. After time deliberating over Analuisa’s existing designs, it was decided that the Scoop Lamp would be most conducive to printing. This was a piece included in her 2022 installation at Stroll Garden Gallery in Los Angeles, where she designed and built a living room out of ceramic, including a chair, floor lamp, wall mounted pieces.

stroll garden, house & garden

House & Garden is a two-artist exhibition of all new works by Lily Clark and Analuisa Corrigan. Conceived as a singular installation, the show welcomes viewers into a home of domestic items rendered in clay by Corrigan and a garden of working ceramic fountains by Clark, complimented by live plant vignettes created by Alice Lam of A.L. BASA. In recontextualizing the familiar, the artists invite a moment of pause to reengage our senses and reconsider our relationship to often overlooked elements of the everyday, both domestic and elemental.
